You are a Syndicate crewmember!

You have been assigned to the SSV Nebuchadnezzar, prototype vessel utilising stolen technology.

Your objective is to weaken NT's presence in this sector. You can go about this in a few ways.

The Syndicate gains influence whenever Nanotrasen loses a fleet, and vice versa. Destroy NT fleets to help the Syndicate gain influence.

Your ship is equipped with a lighthouse beacon which can claim systems for the Syndicate and call in reinforcements. Use it wisely! If you send in Syndicate reinforcements, you risk them being destroyed and the Syndicate losing influence because of this

Ways to win:

While your primary objective is to destroy NT's fleets, you can also destroy the NSV to win! Superstructure crit can totally destroy the NSV and allow you to win...

Another option is to nuke the ship directly. To do that, you must get the nuclear disk.

Good luck, and fly safe. Mentors are on hand to answer questions. If you feel unable to do your job or have to leave at any point, ahelp immediately for assistance!
