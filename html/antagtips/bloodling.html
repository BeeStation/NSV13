You are a bloodling master!

Gather your strength and build an army, ascend to your final form.

You and your thralls can communicate over the hivemind channel, access it via your HUD on the top left of your screen.

You are able to instill life in creatures, making them your obedient slaves. You can infest humans to create ascended thralls, beings of great power. However, be cautious, as they'll retain some free will as a consequence of this.

You start with one thrall already, work together with them over the hivemind channel to build up your strength.

Use your servants well, and utilise them to meet your goals. When you hit critical biomass, you'll become unstoppable.

For further information visit https://nsv.beestation13.com/wiki/view/Bloodling
