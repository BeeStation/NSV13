You have been assigned to defend a top-secret Syndicate outpost!

The base you are stationed on, Bowie Base 2 underneath the desolate wastes of the planet "Tartarus" in the Scorvio system. This base is an important mental reprogramming facility operated by the Syndicate.

You must defend Bowie base 2 at all costs.


Your base is equipped with a self-destruct device just next to the commodore's office.

It can be activated with an Osterhagen key. Keep the Osterhagen key out of enemy hands at all costs, but note that the key CANNOT leave Tartarus under any circumstances.

The commodore of your base will have spawned on the briefing podium ahead of you. You should all formulate a plan to defend and fortify Bowie Base 2.

Tartarus houses a secret airfield, which you can use to defend yourselves against planetary bombardment refer to https://nsv.beestation13.com/wiki/Guide_to_Fighters for information on how to fly them.

The enemy will come from many angles, and there are many attack vectors. Ensure you secure sufficient points of entry to deny the crew access at all costs.
